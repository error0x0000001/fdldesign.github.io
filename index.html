our services

what we provide?

Crown + Bridge

Precise covering for an existing tooth that improves the way a decayed or broken tooth looks and makes it stronger and longer lasting.

Dental Abutments

Highly accurate connector pieces used in restoring the crown to
dental implant.


Surgical Guide

Created by taking impressions on an intraoral scan of the desired surgical implant site as well as a computerguided 3D implant planning system.

Implant Prosthesis

Precise surgical components that interface with the bone of the jaw to support a dental prosthesis such as a crown, bridge, denture, facial prosthesis or to act as an orthodontic anchor.
